The reality

They can see the art.
But they can't hear the story.

Imagine visiting a museum with someone who doesn't speak English. Right now, the best we offer them is a heavy headset, a queue, and three language options.

That's the problem. And that's why Metaxis exists.

1.5 billion people speak English worldwide — meaning over 80% of the global population doesn't. So it's highly likely you have visitors right now who are missing the story.

The technology exists. The intention is there. But the execution is stuck. Consultants don't create, creators don't consult, and builders don't systematise. Nobody is currently doing it all.

The industry is still designing for a default visitor — and that means millions of people are left out of spaces built for everyone. Inclusive design isn't just the right thing to do. It's the smart thing to do.
